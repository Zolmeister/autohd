Auto HD for YouTube (OpenSource)

Check project at GitHub

Video resolution will be set to preferred quality when it is started.
Users and channels may have a featured video on their homepage which is started automatically. This video will be paused right away.
Adjust Volume by top-down scrolling over player area.
The higher the sensitivity, the more scrolling needed for one scroll-tick. Note that scrolling values are system dependent.
Inverts the top-down scrolling. Operating systems and browsers may mean 'up' and 'down' differently.
Volume change by scroll-ticks.
Jump backward or forward in the video by left-right scrolling over player area.
The higher the sensitivity, the more scrolling needed for one scroll-tick. Note that scrolling values are system dependent.
Inverts the left-right scrolling. Operating systems and browsers may mean 'left' and 'right' differently.
Time-steps by scroll-ticks.
